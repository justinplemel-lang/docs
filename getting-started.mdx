## Getting started

Follow this sequence to go from new to productive: understand how it all fits together, learn what Cowork is (where plugins and scheduled tasks run), learn what skills are, install the CS plugin, and connect your tools.

1. **[How it works](/claude/how-it-works)** — From chatting to delegating: how Skills and Plugins turn Claude into a teammate, how the pieces (models, SDK, Connectors, Skills, Plugins) fit together, and **what Cowork is** (the mode where plugins and automation run).

2. **[What are Claude skills?](/skills/what-are-skills)** — What a skill is, why it matters, what's inside a SKILL.md, and how to install one in Claude.

3. **[Install the Federato CS Plugin](/plugins/federato-cs)** — One install for Salesforce updates, meeting prep, and pre-wired Slack, Gmail, Calendar, and Granola.

4. **[Connect your tools](/claude/connections)** — How to connect Slack, Gmail, Google Calendar, and Granola in Claude so the plugin and skills can use your real data.
