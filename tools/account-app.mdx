## Strategic Account Management App

A custom-built React/Supabase application for managing enterprise accounts with AI-powered insights.

## What it does

A centralized hub for everything about your accounts — contracts, relationships, notes, milestones, and AI-generated insights. Built to replace the scattered spreadsheets and docs that don't talk to each other.

## Key features

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="CS AI Assistant" icon="bot">
    Ask questions about your accounts — stakeholder counts, sentiment scores, contract values, upcoming milestones. Generates Salesforce bi-weekly updates from your call notes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notes & call logs" icon="file-text">
    ClickUp-style notes system. Paste Granola transcripts and auto-generate structured notes. Filter by internal vs. external calls. Full history for AI to reference.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Relationship maps" icon="map">
    Track stakeholders with influence scores and sentiment ratings. See at a glance who your champions and detractors are across business units.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contract management" icon="file-check">
    Track contract details, pricing, special terms, renewal dates, and customer tiers. AI-powered PDF upload for auto-extraction (in progress).
  </Card>
  <Card title="TAM calculator" icon="calculator">
    Model expansion scenarios — what if you grew across all product lines? Calculates total addressable market with current discount structures applied.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Business unit intelligence" icon="building">
    Break down accounts by business units — leaders, premium volumes, product lines, underwriter counts. Feeds into relationship and contract views.
  </Card>
</Columns>
