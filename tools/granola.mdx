**Meeting intelligence**

## Granola.ai

AI-powered meeting transcription and note-taking. Rolled out across Sales, Marketing, and Engineering teams.

## How we use Granola

- **Auto-transcription** — Every customer call is automatically transcribed with speaker identification
- **Feed into Account App** — Copy transcripts into the Account Mgmt App notes for AI-powered analysis and Salesforce update generation
- **Meeting summaries** — Get structured summaries with action items, decisions, and key quotes
- **Searchable history** — Find any conversation detail across your entire meeting history

<Tip>
Paste Granola transcripts into the [Account Management App](/tools/account-app) notes tab to generate bi-weekly Salesforce updates with the CS AI Assistant.
</Tip>
