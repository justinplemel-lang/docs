## Install the Federato CS Plugin

One install gives you everything: all four skills, slash commands (`/salesforce-update`, `/meeting-prep`, `/task-sync`), and pre-wired connectors for Slack, Gmail, Calendar, and Granola. Works in both Cowork and regular Claude chat.

<Card title="Federato CS Plugin v0.2.5" icon="download" href="https://github.com/justinplemel-lang/docs/releases/download/v1.0/federato-cs-v0.2.5.plugin.zip">
  Download .plugin — 4 skills, 3 commands, 4 connectors
</Card>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the plugin">
    Click the download card above to get `federato-cs-v0.2.5.plugin.zip`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Cowork">
    In Claude, switch to **Cowork** mode using the toggle at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Go to **Customize → Plugins → + Add** and select your `.plugin` or `.zip` file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your tools">
    Go to **Customize → Connectors** and connect:
    - **Slack** — channel messages and threads
    - **Gmail** — email threads with customers
    - **Google Calendar** — meeting details and attendees
    - **Granola** — meeting transcripts (richest data source)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using it">
    The skills work automatically in both Cowork and Chat. Try `/salesforce-update` in Cowork, or just ask Claude in chat: "Write my bi-weekly update for Accelerant."
  </Step>
</Steps>
