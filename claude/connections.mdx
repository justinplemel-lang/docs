## Connections (Connectors)

Connections give Claude access to your tools and data — Slack, Gmail, Granola, Calendar, and more. Once connected, you can ask Claude to check your calendar, search email, pull from Granola transcripts, or use Slack without leaving the conversation.

### How to add connections

1. In Claude, open **Customize** (left sidebar).
2. Click **Connectors**.
3. Browse or search for the service (e.g. **Slack**, **Gmail**, **Google Calendar**, **Granola**).
4. Follow the prompts to authorize; Claude will have access only to what you approve.

### Why connect these?

| Connection | What Claude can do |
|------------|--------------------|
| **Slack** | Search and reference channels, DMs, and threads when answering you. |
| **Gmail** | Find emails, summarize threads, and draft replies with context. |
| **Google Calendar** | Check your schedule, see meeting details, and prep you for upcoming calls. |
| **Granola** | Pull meeting transcripts and summaries so Claude can reference what was said in past calls. |

With **Skills** and **Connectors** together, Claude can combine your preferences (from skills) with your real data (from connectors) — e.g. "Prep me for my UFG call tomorrow" using calendar + Granola + your preferred format. [See the demo](/claude/connected).
