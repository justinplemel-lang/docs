**Claude**

## When everything's connected

Skills teach Claude *how* to do things. **Connections** (Slack, Gmail, Granola, Calendar) give Claude *access* to your real data and tools. Together, you can ask for things like "Prep me for my call tomorrow" and get calendar, transcripts, and context in one place — without re-explaining everything each time.
