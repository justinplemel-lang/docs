**Feedback**

## Request a skill or feature

Have an idea for a new skill, automation, or tool improvement? Submit it and it'll go into the backlog.

### How to submit

The form will ask you:

1. **What specific task or process** do you propose to automate or enhance with a new Claude skill? (Be specific about the desired output and function.)
2. **Describe the pain point or challenge** associated with doing this task manually today.
3. **How long does it currently take** (in hours/minutes) to complete this task manually in a typical week?
4. **On a scale of 1 to 10**, how critical is it to automate this specific task? (1 = Not critical, 10 = Absolutely essential.)
5. **(Optional)** Supporting documents, screenshots, or flowcharts that explain the manual process — you may upload them in the form.
