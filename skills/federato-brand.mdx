## Slide generation skill (brand CSS)

A Claude skill that generates polished, on-brand Federato PowerPoint decks. Tell Claude what you need — the audience, the goal, and what people should walk out with — and it builds a complete branded presentation with the right colors, fonts, layouts, and icons. No more starting from scratch or copying slides from old decks.

</Info>

Ready to install? Head to the [Install](/install) page to download.

## What's in the skill

### How it works

- **Pre-build discovery** — Claude asks about deck type, audience, slide count, agenda, "walk out with" outcomes, and source material (Granola transcripts, prior decks, etc.) before building anything.
- **Brand colors and fonts baked in** so every slide automatically uses the Federato palette and typography. No manual formatting needed.
- **Crosshair and Pathfinder placement rules** so the signature brand element shows up in the right places (title slides, photo dividers) and never where it shouldn't (behind cards or text).
- **57 professional icons (Lucide line icons)** pre-rendered in brand colors — shields, handshakes, charts, rockets, clipboards, and more. These make slides look designed instead of AI-generated.
- **20+ slide layouts** based on the Accelerant on-site exemplar deck — title slides, section dividers with nature photography, comparison slides, stat cards, discovery question layouts, timelines, three-column breakdowns, scoring matrices, and more.
- **Quality checks built in** — Claude validates brand rules before finalizing so decks come out clean on the first try.

### What changed in this version

- White slide backgrounds with cream cards for better contrast (matches the exemplar)
- Lucide icon library with 57 icons in 3 brand colors (orange, white, brown)
- Crosshair lines no longer cut through cards and text
- Cream-colored Pathfinder on photo section dividers
- Removed accent lines under card headers
- Cleaner layouts with more whitespace
